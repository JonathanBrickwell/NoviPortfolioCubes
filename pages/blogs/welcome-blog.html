Hey, I feel you! But I'll be quick and to the point.

I wanted to make a blog for a long time, but never got around to doing it. Honestly, I felt like it would be too unnecessary because there are already a lot of bloggers.
But as I got into communities like Dev.to & Stack Overflow, I noticed how I liked sharing knowledge & learning from other people by getting feedback on my own work + all those communities left a warm feeling in my belly.
Despite the comments like : ,,You don't know how to traverse a binary tree? OMG, what a noob. Get the f*** outta here!
I was attracted to the community that was hellbent on helping each other on this perpetual ride of not knowing what the hell is going on.

With that said, welcome to my own blog!

The goal with this blog is to share what I know & of course humbly ask of you to corrrect me & educate me on subjects that we share an interest on.

What content can we expect from you?

Addy Osmani changed my perception on JavaScript forever. I'd like to spend some time to deconstruct the JavaScript design patterns from my perspective. Bring closer the concepts that will enable you to create reusable, flexible & clean JavaScript code.
Such concepts can be applied to anything JavaScript related, so you can take what you've learned and apply it in the architecture of your favorite JavaScript framework!

Alongside of all things related to JavaScript, I will discuss concepts of object oriented programming as well as the best practices of functional programming.
I'll try to best to explain how I organise my projects with Git, what role Java plays in my tech stack & what is Docker.
I'll most certainly touch upon Angular, since it's my working tool, and I'll try to explain why it is a good idea to consider it as your JavaScript framework of choice.

I am really looking forward to this & I'm happy I have a platform where I can share all this content.
Hope you'll enjoy what I have to offer & that you'll find it useful.
Take care good people. Code away!
