My music :

Windmoor

Windmoor is an instrumental musical project that implements tribal elements into its music with amplified focus on a more progressive approach.
Hugely inspired by konnakol, indian rhytmical vocabulary, you can expect rhythm driven compositions with heavy accents on using odd time signatures within complexed yet catchy sections.

Cats, Coffee & Guitars

Cats, Coffee & Guitars, or sometimes called C.C.G. is the jazzy/swing inspired musical projects which enables me to write those sweet piano jazz driven sections that I've always loved to hear. It is primarily piano driven backed up by the drums and bass.
This EP provides the listener with catchy jazz melodies, swing driven rhythms and slick piano solos.
