Windmoor is an instrumental one man band.
One of my biggest passions for the past 6 years.
Here I am to finally share the storyline with you.

The Windmoor story started back in 2013. when I was looking for new musical challenges and desired for something different.
At the time, I was going through an immensely hard time. Antisocial behavior and slight depression settled in, fewer and fewer things were successful in making me engaged and awaken in my every day activities and my defenses crumbled.
I needed to express myself through the music. Communicate my hardship and share the experience.

Thus Windmoor was born.

The way I changed, Windmoor changed.
The way I matured, Windmoor matured.
The way I suffered, Windmoor suffered.
We became one.

The story of Windmoor started with a short clip about a friend who moved to London.
I decided to dedicate a song to my dearest friend, composed with nothing but an acoustic guitar and a little bit of synth. Simple yet honest begginings.
Take a listen.
